Introduction

Banking exams are conducted by IBPS, SBI, RBI for recruitment of Clerks, Probationary Officers, Specialist Officers.

Exam Pattern

Prelims + Mains + Interview (for PO/Officer). Clerical posts usually have Prelims + Mains only.

Syllabus

  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Reasoning Ability
  • English Language
  • General Awareness
  • Computer Knowledge

Vacancies

IBPS 2025: 10,000+ Expected across PO, Clerk, SO

Salary

IBPS Clerk: ₹28,000–₹32,000 per month. IBPS PO: ₹52,000–₹55,000 per month.

Eligibility

Graduate in any stream. Age: 20–30 years (varies by category).
