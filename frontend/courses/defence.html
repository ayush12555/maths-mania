Introduction

Defence exams like NDA, CDS, AFCAT, CAPF are conducted by UPSC and other authorities for officer and soldier posts.

Exam Pattern

NDA: Written Exam + SSB Interview CDS: Written Exam + SSB Interview AFCAT: CBT + AFSB CAPF: Written + PET + Interview

Syllabus

  • Mathematics
  • General Ability Test
  • English
  • Current Affairs

Vacancies

NDA 2025: 400+ CDS 2025: 300+ AFCAT 2025: 250+

Salary

Lieutenant: ₹56,100 – ₹1,77,500 CAPF AC: ₹56,100 – ₹1,77,500

Eligibility

NDA: 12th Pass, Age 16.5–19.5 yrs CDS: Graduate, Age 19–25 yrs
