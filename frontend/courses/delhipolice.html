Introduction

Delhi Police conducts recruitment for Constable, Head Constable, and SI under SSC & direct recruitment boards.

Exam Pattern

Written Exam (CBT) + Physical Efficiency Test (PET) + Medical Test.

Syllabus

  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • General Knowledge
  • Reasoning
  • English Language

Vacancies

Delhi Police 2025: 5000+ Expected

Salary

Constable: ₹21,700 – ₹69,100 (Level 3)

Eligibility

10+2 Pass for Constable. Age: 18–25 years (relaxation for reserved categories).
