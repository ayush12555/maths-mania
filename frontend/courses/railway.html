Introduction

Railway Recruitment Boards conduct exams for recruitment in Indian Railways. Major exams: NTPC, ALP, Technician, Group D.

Exam Pattern

Exams conducted in stages: CBT 1, CBT 2, Typing/Skill Test, Document Verification.

Syllabus

  • Mathematics
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Vacancies

RRB NTPC 2025: 35,000+ Expected

Salary

RRB NTPC: Level 2 – ₹19,900 to Level 6 – ₹35,400

Eligibility

Qualification: 10th/12th/Graduate depending on post. Age: 18–33 years.
